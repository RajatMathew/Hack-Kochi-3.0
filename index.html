About Hack Kochi

A 48-hour grand Hackathon organised by IEEE Kochi Hub which will have fun-filled technical games to review you back into hack world.An event that can provide platform and infrastructure for the budding coders across Kerala to boost up their eminent skills in collaboration with ORE. It is an open Hackathon ,free of registration ,where you can be an individual participant or with a group of five. Amazing cash rewards are awaiting the winners.Let's make best out of you.

About ORE

Outreach for Resources in Engineering (ORE) is a 2 month long mentorship programme initiated by IEEE Kochi hub. It had 50 mentors who imparted knowledge on 25 streams in technology to about 500 college students coming from various parts of Kerala. The students were mentored by well-rounded people having immense expertise in the field of technology ,which gave the students a chance to gain insight and make their ideas to fruitful ones.
